Peanut Butter Jelly Time!

This will be the dopest PP&J you'll ever eat... Trust me! Let's get started.

  1. Pick out some good bread and lay two slices on the counter on top of a plate.
  2. Open your brands of peanut butter and jelly.
  3. Take a knife and pull out about 2tbsp of jelly and spread it on one slice of bread.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the peanut butter on the other slice of bread.
  5. Combine the two slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly facing inward.
  6. Don't cut off the crust!!!!!
